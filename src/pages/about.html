ABOUT YONDU

Yondu, a technology solutions company established in 2001, is a subsidiary of Xurpas and Globe Telecom.

Xurpas is one of the largest listed consumer technology companies in Southeast Asia while Globe Telecom is a major provider of telecommunications services in the Philippines.
